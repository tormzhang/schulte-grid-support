Privacy Policy

Schulte Grid is designed as a local training app and does not require account registration.

English

Schulte Grid respects your privacy. The app provides Schulte grid training in an offline, local manner. It does not require account registration, does not operate developer servers for receiving user data, and does not integrate advertising or behavioral analytics platforms.

The app stores training records and app preferences locally on your device. These records are used only to display training history, daily records, settings, and membership-related app features.

In-app purchases, purchase restoration, subscription management, downloads, and updates are provided by Apple. Your device may communicate with Apple services for these functions, and Apple processes related information according to its own terms and privacy policy.

If you delete the app, local app data may be deleted from your device. Deleting the app does not automatically cancel an App Store subscription.
