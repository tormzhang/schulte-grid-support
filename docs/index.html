Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my training data stored?

Training records and app settings are stored locally on your device. The app does not require an account.

How do I manage my subscription?

Open the app, tap Settings, then tap Manage Subscription. You can also manage subscriptions in your Apple ID settings.

How do I restore a purchase?

Open the app, tap Settings, then tap Restore Purchases.

常见问题

训练记录保存在哪里？

训练记录和应用设置保存在你的设备本地，应用不需要注册账号。

如何管理订阅？

打开 App，进入 Settings，然后点击 Manage Subscription。你也可以在 Apple ID 的订阅管理中操作。

如何恢复购买？

打开 App，进入 Settings，然后点击 Restore Purchases。
